Core Values
Core values are developed on the three levels that WHOA and GoodPoint aim to promote wellbeing. Each level is broken down from as internal and external.
  • Personal
    • Internal - GOOD - We are all good people.
    • External - MEANING - We internalize information to create meaning.
  • Interpersonal (Social)
    • Internal - FEEDBACK - We grow from and are guided by feedback
    • External - CONNECTION - We build connection through positive experiences.
  • Community (System)
    • Internal - BALANCE - We strive to maintain and restore balance in our world.
    • External - CHANGE - Be the change! (We can change our world by putting our good thoughts and words into action!)
To the right is a diagram of the 6 core values (orange) and 12 emergent core values that stem from the synthesis of the core value neighbors. The first set of emergent core values (blue) results from the combination of each pair of core values. For example, ask yourself, "What emerges when you combine the idea of GOOD with the idea of MEANING?" We believe that is VIRTUE. The final set of emergent core values (green) arise in a second pass around and are the synthesis of the blue emergent core values, rooted in the core value (orange) from which they emerge. For example, ask yourself, "What is the emerges from the synthesis of VIRTUE and KNOWLEDGE, rooted in MEANING?" We believe that is WISDOM.
